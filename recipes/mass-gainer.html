Mass Gainer

Mass gainer shake with creatine

Great to take with lunch or as a snack. Questionable health benefits. Tastes good though, and it's a consistent source of creatine!

Ingredients

  • Mass gainer (I use Mutant Mass but you can experiment with different options)
  • Creatine
  • Water or milk

Steps

  1. Put 1 scoop of creatine in a glass.
  2. Add a bit of water (this prevents the mass gainer from sticking to the bottom). I'm using water for this recipe but if you'd prefer milk please use milk instead wherever you see water.
  3. Add 1 serving of mass gainer.
  4. Fill up with water.
  5. Stir.
  6. Add additional water if necessary.
