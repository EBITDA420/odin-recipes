Avocado Toast with Smoked Salmon

Delicious avocado spread over toast with smoked salmon on top

Great as a breakfast or lunch, this dish is fast, easy, and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Avocado
  • Smoked salmon
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Toast sourdough bread
  2. Spread chopped up avocado onto bread
  3. Apply salt and black pepper to taste
  4. Cover with smoked salmon
